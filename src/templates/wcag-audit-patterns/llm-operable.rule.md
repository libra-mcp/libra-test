# Rule: Operable (WCAG Principle 2)

## What to check
- All functionality is keyboard operable with logical focus order and no traps.
- Skip links/landmarks support efficient navigation.
- Focus indicators are visible and not obscured (including sticky header cases).
- Time limits and auto-updating motion provide pause/extend controls where needed.
