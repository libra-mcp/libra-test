# Rule: Purposeful Motion Foundations

## What to check
- Motion communicates feedback, orientation, focus, or continuity.
- Use a consistent timing scale: 100-150ms (micro), 200-300ms (small), 300-500ms (medium).
- Prefer easing intent: ease-out for entering, ease-in for exiting, ease-in-out for in-place transitions.
