# Rule: Accessibility and Touch (critical)

## What to check
- Meet baseline accessibility: contrast, focus visibility, keyboard flow, labels, alt text.
- Enforce touch targets >=44x44 and clear tap/click affordances.
- Provide clear async feedback (loading/disabled/error states) near the interaction point.
